About GDG Indore

Google Developer Group Indore is inspired by GTUG/GDG Family . We try to engage student developers, fresh graduates and professionals through our hack events & meetups. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & hackers in and around Indore. The technology awareness is main goal for first few years of the group.
